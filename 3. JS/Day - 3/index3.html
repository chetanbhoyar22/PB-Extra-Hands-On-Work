    Operators in JS

  • 1. Arithmetic Operators
    • +, -, *, /, % (pre & post increment(++) / decrement(--))

  • 2. Comparison (Relational) Operators
    • ==, ===, !=, !==, <, >, >=, <=

  • 3. Logical Operators
    • &&, ||, !

  • 4. Assignment Operators
    • =, +=, -= , *=, /=, %=

  • 5. Ternary Operators
    • condition(val1==val2) ? 'true' : 'false'

  • 6. Bitwise Operators
    • &, |, ^, ~, <<, >>, >>>


    Note - Example are executed in js file.
