Loops in JS

1. for...loop

When you know exactly how many numbers of times the loop is going to be executed then we will use for loop.

  • I. forward loop
  • II. backward loop

2. while...loop

When you don't know exactly how many numbers of times the loop is going to be executed then we will use while loop.

3. do...while loop

The do...while statement creates a loop that executes a specified statement until the test condition evaluates to false. The condition is evaluated after executing the statement, resulting in the specified statement executing at least once.

Note - Example are executed in js file.
